## ⚡ Key Highlights & Built-in Features

<details open>
<summary><b>📊 Click to Expand/Collapse Features Showcase</b></summary>
<br>

* 🛡️ **`🔒 Seamless Gateway Login`** Features a secure, beautifully animated **Cyberpunk Entry Screen** to protect your personalized trading workspace from unauthorized access.
  
* 📈 **`📊 Live Profit/Loss Analytics`** Automatically tracks and calculates your **Weekly & Monthly PnL** alongside win-rate percentages with Excel-like high efficiency and real-time computation.
  
   **`📁 Embedded Notes & PDF Viewer`** Zero friction! View your trading rules, notes, and study PDFs **directly inside the web interface** without any external Google Drive redirection.
  
   **`⚙️ Advanced Configuration Mapping`** Custom dynamic settings to map your preferred **Currency Pairs, Trading Methods** (e.g., MSS, CISD), and specific trading sessions instantly.
  
   **`📸 Daily Evidence Row`** Instantly upload, manage, and preview chart screenshots directly inside any specific trading day row for quick visual review and journaling.
  
   **`💾 Safe Auto-Save & Backup`** Zero data loss guarantee with automated background cloud saving and a secure **1-click local `.json` file backup** system.

</details>
